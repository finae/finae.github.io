FINAE Labs Inc.

Making AI work for everyone.

We build affordable AI agents that solve real business problems. Our first product, Gins.AI, brings autonomous marketing to small businesses - handling everything from content creation to campaign optimization.

Our name comes from SFINAE in C++ ("Substitution Failure Is Not An Error"). Just like SFINAE keeps trying different approaches until finding the right match, our agents persist through challenges to find optimal solutions. This isn't just our technology - it's our culture. We see failures not as setbacks, but as valuable datapoints guiding us toward better solutions.

Based in Mountain View, CA, we're a team of engineers and researchers making AI technology accessible to all.
